ABOUT US

Citizens Against Tyranny is a nonpartisan coalition united by an unwavering commitment to defend the Constitution, civil liberties, and democratic governance. We reject the notion that government exists to rule—it exists to serve the people.

OUR MISSION

We confront threats to democracy head-on. Grounded in constitutional ideals, we challenge authoritarianism, corruption, and the abuse of power—regardless of political party or position. Through bold civic engagement and relentless advocacy, we fight to:

  • Defend the rule of law against manipulation and erosion.
  • Protect fundamental rights from state overreach and corporate exploitation.
  • Expose and resist authoritarian actors who seek to dismantle democratic norms.
  • Demand truth and transparency from institutions, leaders, and media platforms.
  • Break the grip of concentrated power—political, technological, or economic—that silences dissent and fuels inequality.
  • Mobilize citizens to stand firm for liberty, justice, and accountable leadership.

As part of this mission, we are unequivocally committed to holding Donald t(R)ump accountable—including through impeachment—for his attacks on democratic institutions and the rule of law.

OUR STAND

We sound the alarm against modern tyranny in all its forms. We reject the anti-democratic tactics of Donald t(R)ump, Elon Musk, political enablers, and oligarchs who use influence to spread disinformation, attack institutions, and normalize extremism. Democracy is not a given—it must be defended with vigilance, courage, and clarity.
