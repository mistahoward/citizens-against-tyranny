CONNECTIONS

Allies and partner organizations in the fight for democracy.

Constitution Corner

Rooted in America's heartland, Constitution Corner emerges as a grassroots initiative dedicated to upholding and safeguarding the United States Constitution. Their mission? To rally citizens to challenge government overreach, stand up for equal treatment under the law, and push for transparency in governance.

They are focused on building trust in public institutions and inspiring hope for America's future, working tirelessly towards a more equitable and accountable society. Constitution Corner strives to make a meaningful impact on the country's democratic landscape by creating strategies that promote these values.

The Southwest Missouri Democrats

The Southwest Missouri Democrats are a regional branch of the Missouri Democratic Party. They are focused on engaging with and organizing Democrats in the southwest region of Missouri, which includes Jasper and Greene counties. Their goal is to amplify the voice of progressives in the area and work towards turning Missouri blue, one election at a time. They also support candidates and provide resources for voters, such as helping people register to vote and providing endorsements for candidates and propositions.
