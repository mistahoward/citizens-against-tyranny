REJECT DICTATORSHIP

Citizens Against Tyranny is a nonpartisan coalition dedicated to defending the Constitution, protecting civil liberties, and preserving democratic governance. We believe government exists to serve, not rule, the people. In an era of rising authoritarianism, disinformation, and unchecked power, we stand firm in confronting threats to democracy from all sides. Our mission is clear: uphold the rule of law, safeguard fundamental rights, demand transparency, and challenge the forces—political, corporate, or technological—that endanger liberty and justice. We reject the extremism and anti-democratic influence of figures like Donold t(R)ump, Elon Musk, and their enablers, and we call on all citizens to join us in the vital work of protecting truth, accountability, and constitutional freedom for future generations.

Our goal is to unite Americans across every state, background, and political belief in a shared commitment to liberty, accountability, and community. In a time of deep division, we believe it is still possible to come together—not in uniformity, but in solidarity around our core democratic values. By building bridges instead of burning them, we hope to help to create a national movement rooted in civic respect, mutual responsibility, and a common purpose: to restore the people’s voice and power in shaping a just, free, and inclusive future.
